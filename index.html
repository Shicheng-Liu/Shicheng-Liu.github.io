About me

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Julian Shun and Dr. Yada Zhu.
Previously, I obtained my PhD at Pennsylvania State University in 2025, advised by Prof. Minghui Zhu.
Before moving to Pennsylvania, I obtained the B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2020.

My research interests are centered around reinforcement learning, explainable machine learning, and language model post-training.

  • Reinforcement Learning: Algorithm design for reward shaping, inverse reinforcement learning, reinforcement learning from human feedback, application to robotics and security

  • Explainable machine learning: Algorithm design for explaining RL agents and leveraging the explanation to improve agent performance.

  • language model post-training: Reinforcement learning for language model reasoning, agentic reinforcement learning for langauge model fine-tuning under complex and multi-turn interaction scenarios
